Music Recommender
In Fox Lab, we had data of over 1,000 participants, each of whom listened to 400 songs. Of those songs, 320 were the same and 80 varied. In this project, I built a model using Singular Value Decomposition and Principle Component Analysis to predict a new user's top three songs using only five songs. The ratings vary from a scale of 1 (hate it) to 7 (love it). You can give it a go!

Feedback Is All You Need
Over the past three years, Dr. Pascal Wallisch has asked students to give feedback after every week or every lecture. In this analysis, I analyzed over 10,000 student responses varying across three years (six semesters) to find trends. Our goal was to find out what works and what does not. I also fit a SVC model to predict whether based on previous weeks, we could predict if a student would find a week later in the semester 'confusing' and our model was 84% accurate.
